> **A recorded decision (ADR-style).** Captures a choice and its context so future work
> doesn't relitigate it. Goes `draft → final` when the decision is settled.

## Context

The situation and forces at play that required a decision.

## Decision

What was decided, stated in the active voice.

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder as a result — including security, policy, and verification
implications.

## Alternatives Considered

Options weighed and why they were not chosen.
