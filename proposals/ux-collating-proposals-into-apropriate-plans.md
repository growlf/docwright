## Problem

Many times I have noticed that I will (or with my team, it happens frequently) create proposals that either overlap an existing plan or proposal and it would be less time to process if we caught that overlap or redundancy when we create the proposal or plan. 

Sometimes, when creating a plan, it might be best to review other outstanding related proposals or plans to see whether combining them (or moving components therof) into a plan would be more efficient - then documenting the change and new hierarchy with in the affected documents.

I am looking for a strategic overview layer when creating or promoting a proposal or working on an existing plan.  This could also help set a reasonable priority and hierarchy for plans based on their connection to other plans and proposals already in the pipeline.
